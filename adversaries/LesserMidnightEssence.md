# Lesser Midnight Essence

## Introduction

Lesser Midnight Essences are smaller, weaker manifestations of Re-Shephir's power. Where a full Midnight Essence can mimic human forms and violence with unsettling precision, these lesser versions are crude and instinct-driven -- little more than slicks of animate darkness that cling to walls and ceilings, waiting to drop on the unwary.

They lack the size and strength of their larger counterparts, but what they lack individually they make up for in numbers. Re-Shephir produces them quickly and in quantity, sending them skittering through vents, cracks, and narrow passages where larger Midnight Essences cannot follow.

### Features

**Amorphous.** The Lesser Midnight Essence can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide.

**Fear of Lightweaving.** If a character uses the Illumination surge while within 60 feet of the Lesser Midnight Essence, the Lesser Midnight Essence gains a disadvantage on attack rolls until the end of its next turn.

**Wall Lurker.** The Lesser Midnight Essence has advantage on Stealth tests when pressed against a wall, ceiling, or floor. It can use this to ambush unsuspecting targets.

### Actions

**Strike: Formed Weapon.** Attack +6, reach 5 ft., one target. *Graze:* 2 (1d4) impact or keen damage. *Hit:* 8 (1d4 + 6) impact or keen damage.

### Tactics

**Lesser Midnight Essences** lurk in cracks and crevices, flattening against walls and ceilings until prey passes beneath them. They attack in swarms, overwhelming targets with numbers rather than individual strength. When Lightweaving is used against them, they scatter.
