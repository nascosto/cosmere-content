### Assassin's Crossbow (paired)
A pair of concealed wrist-mounted crossbows strapped to the forearms, designed for covert operations. The crossbows are compact enough that the wielder's hands remain free to hold items, wield other weapons, or interact with objects normally. Both crossbows can be fired together for 1 action (still costs the normal focus for each Strike). Each crossbow has the Loaded [1] trait and **cannot be reloaded during combat** — once fired, they are spent until the wielder has time to reload outside of combat.

### Invested Sap Gloves
Weighted knuckle gloves infused with Investiture-disrupting properties. The gloves add 1d4 impact damage to any unarmed attack (stacking with normal unarmed damage).

- **Charges (2 charges).** When the wearer hits an Invested being with an unarmed attack, the gloves automatically expend 1 charge to drain 1 Investiture from the target. This effect triggers automatically and cannot be controlled by the wielder. The charge is not expended if the target has no Investiture.
- **Recharging.** Standard recharging rules (requires Investiture or gems).

### Knights Radiant Leather
Finely crafted leather armor bearing the sigils of the Knights Radiant. Designed for Radiants who don't specialize in warfare — scholars, diplomats, and scouts who need practical field armor that is also presentable enough for Radiant duties.

### Knights Radiant Breastplate
A sturdier chestpiece crafted with Radiant craftsmanship, bearing the same quality and distinction as the lighter leather variant. Suited for Radiants who face more direct combat but still serve in public-facing roles.

### Chain Jezzerant
A refined chain mail with small plates riveted between layers of fabric, giving it a cleaner, more presentable appearance than standard chain. Favored by those who operate in social settings while remaining armored — infiltrators, courtiers, and disciplined operatives.

## Custom Fabrials

### Compressor Fabrial (Upgraded)
**Tier 1 Unique Fabrial — 3 charges**

This fabrial can cause flexible objects it touches to become rigid. As an action, make an Agility test against the Physical defense of a target within your reach. On a success, you can spend 1 or more charges to cause the target to become **Immobilized** and to gain a disadvantage on all physical tests for that many rounds.

- **Upgrade (built-in):** The target becomes Immobilized instead of the standard Slowed effect.
- **Recharging.** Standard recharging rules (requires Investiture or gems).

## Training Rewards

### Mountainstance (Hammer Stance)
*Learned through training with a hammer expert.*

A traditional fighting stance adapted for hammers, focused on holding ground against an offensive assault. The wielder plants their feet and uses the weapon's weight to deflect and absorb incoming strikes.

**Requirements:** Must be wielding a hammer or warhammer.

**Activation:** Spend an action to enter Mountainstance.

**While in Mountainstance:**
- Gain +2 Physical defense.
- You are Slowed.
- The stance persists until you choose to drop it (no action required to end).
